Pill Schedule

This website shows the process behind the application. You have access to diagrams and scheme to understand how we create the project.

Description

The goal of this of project is to achieve an application that notice user about their medical prescription.
It's not always easy to remember when to take your medication and how many doses you must absorb.
Here comes the Pill Schedule : you receive a notification each time you need to take your medication and how many pills you have to take
The app comes with easy and intuitive design that allow to add a prescription and to export as convinient format (pdf, excel...) the history.
